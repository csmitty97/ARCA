China is using AI and 3D printing to build a 590-foot-tall dam without the need for human workers, scientist say

  • Chinese scientists say they're 3D printing a 590-foot-tall dam by 2024 using AI and robots.

  • The project will use an AI system with unmanned trucks, bulldozers, rollers and other equipment.

  • The researchers say their method eliminates human error and safety concerns for workers.

China is poised to build a hydropower dam in two years using artificial intelligence, contrustrion robots, and zero human labor, scientists involved in the project said.
